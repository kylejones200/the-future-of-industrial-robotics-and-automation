## Business context

SMEs are crucial to the economy, accounting for many jobs and economic growth. By adopting industrial robotics and automation, SMEs can increase their competitiveness, improve production efficiency, and reduce costs.

Several factors drive the increased adoption of industrial robotics and automation in SMEs:

1. [The cost of these technologies is decreasing as they become more widely available and production volumes increase.] 2. [Developing more user-friendly software and hardware makes it easier for SMEs to implement and operate these technologies.] 3. [Because of the growth of the Internet of Things (IoT) and cloud-based computing, SMEs may now use the potential of industrial robots and automation without investing in costly on-premise equipment.]
